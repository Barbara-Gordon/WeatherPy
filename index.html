Lattitude vs the Weather

The purpose of this project was to determine whether the weather changes as your approach the equator. The goal was to determine if factors such as temparture, humidity, cloud cover, and windspeed show predictable patterns based on lattitude. After compiling a randomized list of 500 cities, we used the OpenWeatherMap API to gather our data.

After pulling the data, we used MatPlotLib to plot the various weather patterns -temperature, wind speed, humidity, and cloud cover - of a given city against the latitude. This site aims to provide an overview of our analysis, including visualizations and observations, as well as a look at the source data.
